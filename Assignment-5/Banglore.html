Bangalore

Bangalore, also known as Bengaluru, is the capital of the Indian state of Karnataka. It is known as the "Silicon Valley of India" due to its role as the nation's leading information technology (IT) exporter. The city is also famous for its parks, nightlife, and a pleasant climate. Key attractions include Lalbagh Botanical Garden, Cubbon Park, Bangalore Palace, and the vibrant shopping and dining scenes.

The primary language in Bangalore is Kannada, spoken by the majority of the population. English is also widely used, especially in business and education. Due to the city's diverse population, other languages like Tamil, Telugu, Hindi, and Malayalam are also commonly spoken, reflecting Bangalore's cosmopolitan nature.
